My Story

Work and Education:

After spending time as a mutual fund accountant, a domestic engineer, and an elementary school and art museum volunteer, I embarked on the path to become a UI Designer. The aspects of combining typography, creativity and organization with structure and accountability, as well as utilizing elements of psychology - a top 5 favorite subject of mine, enormously excited and inspired me to make this my career transition.

Additionally, editing information,rearranging/organizing spaces (digital or physical) and designing layouts are experiences that I find immensely fulfilling and satisfying. I have a Master of Science degree in Accounting, a Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics with a minor in Business Administration, and a certificate in User Interface Design.

Interests/Hobbies:

Cats - I have 3 adorable rescue fluffballs and featured them in my Pet Directory Plus project. Audiobooks are my chore, exercise and hobby companion. I mostly listen to science fiction and fantasy, but also select and highly acclaimed fiction and cozy mystery. The hobbies in which I engage this glorious method of entertainment include, but are not limited to, building legos, drawing, painting, collaging, crafting and photography. Solving puzzles, whether jigsaw, coding or optimal communication through written and aestetic organization and arrangement, is my favorite.
